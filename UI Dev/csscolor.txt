Colors:==================
1)RGB (Red,Green,Blue):
The rgb Color Value is specified with ()function.

3)HSL (Hue,Saturation,Lightness):
hue is a degree on the color while (0 to 360) an red is 120 and blue 240.
saturation is a percentage value 0% means a shade of gray & 100% is the full of clors.
the lightness als0 define 0% to 100%.
